About me
Hi! I’m Ivy Fawcett, a young filmmaker and visual storyteller currently finishing my first year at Royal Holloway, University of London, where I study Film Production with a focus on cinematography and editing. My journey into filmmaking has been a bit unconventional— I graduated high school a year and a half early, before taking the leap and moving to the UK to pursue higher education. I have since graduated from an international foundation year program with Study Group at RHUL. Prior to university, I immersed myself in the world of theater, taking on roles both on and off the stage. From directing, to acting and sound design, to costuming and stage manager, I love to bring stories to life, and I am beyond passionate about my work. That passion for visual storytelling naturally led me to film, where I thrive behind the camera and behind the scenes.
I am currently working on a mix of student films and personal projects, working as a director, cinematographer, boom operator, producer, and editor, always looking for new ways to experiment, learn, and grow as a filmmaker.
